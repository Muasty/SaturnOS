System Architecture

Saturn OS operates on a core of hydrogen and helium. Unlike rocky planetary systems, our architecture is fluid, massive, and incredibly fast. At the heart of the system lies a rocky core surrounded by deep layers of metallic liquid.

The Hexagon Protocol

Located at the North Pole, the Hexagon Protocol is a permanent atmospheric phenomenon. It is a six-sided jet stream that serves as the system's primary storm-management engine, maintaining stability across the northern hemisphere.

Ring Partitioning

The system's storage is partitioned into thousands of icy rings. These rings are not solid, but composed of billions of individual particles ranging from dust to boulders, all synchronized in a perfect orbital dance.
