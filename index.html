Saturn: The Ringed Jewel

Exploring the sixth planet from the Sun.

Overview

Saturn is the second-largest planet in our solar system and is best known for its spectacular, complex system of icy rings. Like Jupiter, Saturn is a gas giant, composed mostly of hydrogen and helium.

The Ring System

While other giants have rings, Saturn's are the most bright and visible. They are made of billions of individual pieces of ice and rock, ranging in size from tiny dust grains to chunks as large as mountains.
